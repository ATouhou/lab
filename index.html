Corpus Data View

stable features:

  • Integration of corpus grammar info, word-by-word meaning, Buckwalter encoded quran.
  • corpus grammar: see root & lemma info word-by-word on mouseover.
    Click on either to search. other grammar features like part of speech, verb form, tense, declension etc.
alpha features:
  • Near Synonyms/Mutaraadifaat. sources: (1) Nouman Ali Khan's Near Synonyms handout, (2) Mutaraadifaat urdu book.
  • Newly occuring words + # of occurences of each word.
  • Teaching aid: see applicable hand icon which conveys pictographically the gender/tense/person. (see www.understandquran.com)
  • more features under test.
